# AWD

---

Design and construction of an Accelerated Weight Drop system optimized for use in polar environments.

- Safe to operate  
- Low weight and suitable for transport by helicopter  
- Optimized for soft surfaces, such as snow or porous firn  
- Mechanical operation, with the option of using off-the-shelf electrical operation; e.g., power tool  
- No DG transport  
- Minimal environmental impact  
- The energy of a single impact over 500 J, preferably closer to 1000 J  
- Generating both S and P waves  
- Easy maintenance  
- Working in temperatures down to -30°C  
- Modular  
- Open design
